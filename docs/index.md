## 📣 About The Project

Welcome to the `badgeshield` package! This package allows you to generate custom SVG badges for your GitLab projects. It's ideal for creating badges for build statuses, contributors, and much more.


## 🔥 Features

- **Multiple templates** – Choose from rectangular, circle, and framed styles.
- **Rich color palette** – Use built-in color enums or provide custom hex values.
- **Adaptive layout** – Text widths are measured with font metrics (via Pillow) for consistent spacing.
- **Logos, links, and titles** – Enhance your badges with branding and accessible metadata.
- **Batch processing** – Generate many badges concurrently from a JSON configuration.
- **CLI & API** – Automate badge generation from scripts or integrate with CI pipelines.
